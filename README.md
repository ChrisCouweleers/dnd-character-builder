# Forge & Fable

A free, web-based D&D 5th Edition character builder. Create characters, roll dice, and manage your party — all saved to the cloud.

**Live at [dnd-char-builder.vercel.app](https://forge-n-fable.vercel.app)**

## What It Does

- **Build characters** step by step — pick your race, class, background, roll ability scores, and choose skill proficiencies
- **Interactive character sheets** — click any ability or skill to instantly roll a d20 check
- **Track HP** — visual health bar with heal, damage, and full heal controls
- **Manage equipment** — add items and toggle what's equipped
- **Roll dice** — built-in roller for d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20, and d100
- **Cloud saves** — sign up with email and your characters sync across any device

## Supported Content

- 10 races with subraces (Human, Elf, Dwarf, Halfling, Half-Elf, Half-Orc, Tiefling, Gnome, Dragonborn)
- 12 classes (Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)
- 13 backgrounds
- All 18 skills with automatic modifier calculations
- Proficiency bonuses, saving throws, and passive perception

## How to Use

1. Go to [dnd-char-builder.vercel.app](https://forge-n-fable.vercel.app)
2. Create a free account with your email
3. Click **Create Character** and follow the five-step wizard
4. Open your character sheet to roll checks, track HP, and manage gear during sessions
